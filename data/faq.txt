========================================
GROWTHFORGE MEDIA - FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
========================================

General
-------
Q: What makes GrowthForge Media different from other agencies?
A: We specialize exclusively in local businesses. Instead of vanity metrics like followers or likes, we focus 100% on generating qualified, exclusive leads. Plus, everything is transparent with real-time dashboards and a 90-day guarantee.

Q: Do I need a large marketing budget to work with you?
A: No. Our Starter package requires just a $500/month ad spend. We've designed our pricing to be accessible for local businesses looking to test the waters before scaling up.

Q: How long does it take to see results?
A: Most clients see their first leads within 48 to 72 hours of campaign launch. The first 14 days are typically our testing phase, and you'll see full optimization and consistent predictable lead flow by month 2.

Pricing & Contracts
-------------------
Q: Am I locked into a long-term contract?
A: No. We recommend a 90-day commitment to allow the platforms algorithms to fully optimize, but we don't lock you in. You can cancel our Starter package with just 30 days notice.

Q: Do I pay the ad spend to you or to Facebook/Instagram?
A: You pay the ad spend directly to the platforms (Meta/Facebook) using your own credit card. You only pay GrowthForge Media our flat management fee. This ensures 100% financial transparency.

Services & Process
------------------
Q: Do I need to create the ad images or videos?
A: No, our team handles all creative development, including ad copy, images, and standard videos. If you have your own photos or videos, we love using them, but it isn't required.

Q: What happens to the leads once they are generated?
A: Leads can be delivered instantly via email, SMS text message, or routed directly into your existing CRM system (like GoHighLevel, HubSpot, or Salesforce).

Results & Guarantees
--------------------
Q: What happens if you don't hit the guaranteed lead targets?
A: Under our 90-Day Guarantee, if we don't generate at least 20-30 qualified leads per month (or the custom target we agreed on), we continue working on your account at a 50% reduced management fee until we hit that target.

Q: How do I know the leads are actually qualified?
A: We use custom lead-generation forms or dedicated landing pages with qualifying questions specific to your industry. We don't use simple "name/email" forms that generate low-intent clicks.
